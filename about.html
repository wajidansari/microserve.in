About Microserve.in

Your trusted partner for computer and laptop repairs since 2015

Our Story

Founded in 2015, Microserve.in began as a small computer repair shop in Bangalore with a simple mission: to provide honest, reliable, and affordable repair services to our community.

What started as a one-person operation has grown into a team of certified technicians serving thousands of satisfied customers across the city. We've expanded our services, but our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction remains unchanged.

Today, we're proud to be one of Bangalore's most trusted computer and laptop repair centers, known for our technical expertise and exceptional service.

Our Approach

At Microserve.in, we believe in a transparent, customer-first approach to computer repairs:

  • Free diagnostics and honest assessments
  • No repair without your approval
  • High-quality replacement parts with warranty
  • Clear pricing with no hidden fees
  • 6-month warranty on all repairs

We treat every device as if it were our own, and every customer as part of our family.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To provide exceptional computer repair services that solve our customers' technology problems quickly, affordably, and with the highest level of professionalism. We aim to build lasting relationships based on trust, quality workmanship, and outstanding customer service.

Our Vision

To become Bangalore's most trusted and recommended computer repair service by continually improving our skills, investing in the latest technology, and maintaining our commitment to honest, transparent service. We envision a future where no one has to struggle with technology problems alone.
